Akio Toyoda

豊田章男

Akio Toyoda (豊田 章男, Toyoda Akio, born 3 May 1956) is a Japanese business executive and the current Chairman and Former president & CEO of Toyota Motor Corporation. He is a great-grandson of the industrialist, Sakichi Toyoda, and a grandson of both the founder of Toyota Motors, Kiichiro Toyoda, and the founder of the Takashimaya department stores corporation, Shinshichi Iida. On 1 April 2023, Toyoda will step down as Toyota president and become chairman of the board.
